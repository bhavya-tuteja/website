IMPORTANT TERMS IN GRID SYSTEM

  • Grid Container has three rows and three columns
  • Grid lines are horizontal lines and vertical lines
  • Grid Cell is a square created by cutting lines (hor & vert)
  • Grid Track is the space between 2 adjecent lines
  • Grid Item is direct descendant/childen of grid container.
  • Items can overlap each other. We cn use z-index to control their stacking order.
