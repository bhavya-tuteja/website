SVG INTRODUCTION:

Intrtoduction of SVG From INitial Step

  • INTORDUCTION: SVG stands for 'Scalable Vector Graphics'.
  • What is know-how: Knowledge of html & Basic XML
  • RECOMENDATION: SVG is a W3C recomendation
  • ITEGERATION: SVG integrates with other W3C standards such as DOM and XSL
  • SVG (Second Edition ): SVG 1.1 (Second Edition) became a W3C Recommendation on 16.08.2011
  • NO LOSS OF QUALITY: SVG graphics do not lose quality on zoomed or resized
  • TEXT EDITOR: SVG can be created with any text editor.
  • INKSCAPE: SVG images with a drawing program, like Inkscape
